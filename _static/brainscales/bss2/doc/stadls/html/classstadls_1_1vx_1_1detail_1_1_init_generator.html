Detailed Description

Generator for an initialization of the xBoard and the chip up to allowing access to all digital settings and systime annotation of response data.

If desired, the highspeed link is enabled (defaults to true). If desired, the CapMem is enabled along with its reference generator and the selection of internal synapse bias currents (defaults to false).
