◆ i_bias_source_follower

AnalogValueVariant lola::vx::v1::AtomicNeuron::LeakReset::i_bias_source_follower

Bias current of the source follower in the leak/reset circuit.

Both the leak and reset potential are connected via this source follower.

The voltage is dropped by approximately 0.6 V. Higher bias currents mean slightly higher voltage drops.
