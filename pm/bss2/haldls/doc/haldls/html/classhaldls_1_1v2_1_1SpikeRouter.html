Delay const &  delay

Enable "squeeze" mode, where every spike received in a specified interval is combined into a single packet with a fixed spike address.

Note
In the verilog implementation this mode is called "bypass" mode. It is implemented by or-ing the spike vector (target synapse drivers) of all received spikes and assumes a fixed mapping from neurons to synapse drivers, e.g. spike events of neuron 0, 1, 2, … will be sent to synapse drivers 0, 1, 2, …. After a specified delay, a single spike with a fixed address will be emitted (as a single packet) to all synapse drivers, whose corresponding neuron spiked at least once in that interval.
